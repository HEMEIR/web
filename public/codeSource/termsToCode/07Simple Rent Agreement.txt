Clauses:

1. Initial Payment and Security Deposit: Prior to the Start Date, the Lessee, B, shall pay to the Lessor, A, the first payment for the rent plus the Security Deposit.

2. Regular Rent Payments: Commencing from the Start Date, the Lessee shall make rent payments every ten days up to and including the End Date.

3. Lease Extension: Prior to the End Date, the Lessee may input the number of days for lease extension and make the corresponding Extension Payment (this process may be repeated).

4. Lessor's Decision on Extension: Following the Lessee's request for extension as per Clause 3, the Lessor shall decide whether to agree to the extension (this process may be repeated).
   - If the Lessor agrees to the extension, the End Date shall be amended accordingly.
   - If the Lessor does not agree to the extension, the Extension Payment shall be refunded to the Lessee.

5. Contract Termination and Refund of Security Deposit: After the End Date, the Lessor shall conclude the contract, return the Security Deposit to the Lessee, and this Agreement shall be deemed complete.
