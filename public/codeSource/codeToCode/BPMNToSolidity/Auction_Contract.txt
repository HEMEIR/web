Key Elements and Terms of the Auction Contract
Seller: The seller is <seller>, registered at <Address1>, contactable via <Num1>.
Auction Start Time: <auctionStartTime>.
Bidding Duration: <biddingTime>.
Auction End Time: <auctionStartTime> + <biddingTime>.
Minimum Bid Price: <lowprice>.
Highest Bid Price: Initially set as <lowprice>.
Highest Bidder: <highestBidder>.

Seller' s Rights and Obligations
Obligation to endAuction: If the auction end time (<auctionEndTime>) has passed and the auction has not ended, the seller must conclude the auction.
Right to receivePayment: If bids were placed, the seller receives the highest bid amount.
Bidder' s Rights and Obligations
Right to placeBid: If the auction is active (now <= auctionEndTime) and the bid amount exceeds the current highest price, the bidder may place a bid.
Obligation to payBid: The bidder must pay the bid amount upon placing a bid.
Right to refundPreviousBid: If a higher bid is placed, the previous highest bidder is refunded their bid amount.
Right to highestBid: If the bidder wins the auction, they are entitled to claim the item after the auction ends.
